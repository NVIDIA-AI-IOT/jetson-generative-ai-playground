Nurgaliyev Shakhizat demonstrates a locally-hosted Blind Assistant Device running on Jetson AGX Orin 64GB Developer Kit for realtime image-to-speech translation.

The system uses vision-language models to describe the environment and convert descriptions to speech, helping visually impaired users understand their surroundings.
